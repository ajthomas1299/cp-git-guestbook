This week I learned the value of a testimonial!

Qazi, one of my mentor's at Clever Programmer, told me to think of a testimonial as $3,000 in your hand.

Don't be afraid to ask for them!

If you have taught someone one thing, whether it be family or friends or an aquaintance, they can give you a testimonial.

Help them write one!

Give them this general testimonial outline to help them write one. It doesn't have to be long or flashy, ask them:

  1. Where were you before working with me?
  2. Where were you after working with me?
  3. What was working with me like?
  4. What would you say to someone who is on the fence about working with me?
